BATESVILLE POLICE DEPARTMENT

Incident Report #042-1957

Date: April 15, 2025

Subject: Unknown Male, Approx. 19 years old

Name: Unknown / No Identification Found

Location: Near Downtown Batesville, MS

Reporting Officer: Sgt. Marcus Reynolds

Summary

At approximately 22:35 hours on April 14, 2025, officers were dispatched to the intersection of Main Street and Magnolia Avenue following reports of a disturbance involving a male subject exhibiting erratic and violent behavior.

Upon arrival, officers encountered the subject—male, approximately 19 years old, disheveled, unresponsive to identification requests. The individual appeared agitated and distressed, vocalizing incoherent phrases referencing "the shadows beneath" and "voices from the creek."

Subject was observed engaging in self-harm, repeatedly striking his head and shouting unintelligible commands. Attempts to communicate were unsuccessful; the individual’s speech was fragmented and delusional.

Due to the subject’s aggressive behavior and risk to himself and others, officers secured the scene and administered physical restraint.

Incident Details

- No identification or personal belongings were found on the subject.
- Witnesses report seeing the individual wandering aimlessly through residential areas earlier in the day.
- Mental health crisis teams were dispatched; subject was transported to Batesville Psychiatric Facility for emergency evaluation.
- Subject refused cooperation, exhibiting signs consistent with acute psychosis.
- Multiple statements from witnesses indicate the subject muttered references to "the river calling" and "the forgotten ones waiting."
- Officers noted an unusual pattern of scratches and bruises in symmetrical formations on the subject’s forearms.

Follow-Up Actions

- Ongoing investigation to determine subject’s identity and possible connection to recent missing person reports in Batesville.
- Coordination with mental health services for long-term assessment.
- Community alert issued for any information regarding unidentified individuals matching subject’s description.

Officer Notes

This incident raises concerns about potential underlying causes, including possible exposure to unknown environmental factors or psychological trauma. Subject’s behavior remains unpredictable; continued caution advised.
