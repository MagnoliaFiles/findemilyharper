Missing: Emily Harper

Last seen in Batesville, Mississippi - March 5, 2025

Case Summary

Emily Harper, 24, vanished after leaving her job at the local diner downtown. She was wearing a dark jacket and jeans, carrying a small leather-bound notebook. Witnesses report seeing her near the old train tracks late in the evening.

Family and friends are desperate for information. If you have seen Emily or know anything, please contact Batesville Police Department.

Clues Found

Investigators found Emily's notebook, containing strange symbols and fragmented journal entries. Here's a snippet:

"The key lies beneath the rusted bridge. Watch the stars on 03/15."
